Printer Setup


1. Pluging in a printer will prompt a dialog if detected and attempt to find a matching driver. (If your driver was pluged in during your inital install you may not see a prompt, you may refer to step 3 below.)

2. If a printer and driver has been detected you will see this dialog. (Note, the dialog should appear in uper right of your screen)


3. You can further configure or manage printing jobs or printers by going to printing located in Menu> System> Printing.


4. Once you open the Printing menu it will bring you to a simple dialog to select view and manage options related to your printer.

5. You can find options for a printer by highlighting (single clicking) on the icon of the printer you wish to manage.


6. If a printer if not listed in the window after opening it you will need to plug in the device and click on the + sign located below the menu.
